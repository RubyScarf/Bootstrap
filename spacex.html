Overview



SpaceX, a private company, committed to making humanity multiplanetary has helped advance space travel. CEO of SpaceX, Elon Musk, risked all he had to get SpaceX going. His efforts led SpaceX to be well known and led him to being the richest person in the world.



SpaceX's Grasshopper was the first prototype to attempt to land rockets. Last Launch: October 7, 2013

Falcon 1 became their biggest ambition yet. After a total of 3 fails, SpaceX achieved orbit and land. Last Launch: June 14, 2009

Falcon 9 has completed 168 successful mission out of a total of 188. It has been used on the historical Demo Dragon Mission in May of 2020. Lastest Launch: Nov 2, 2022

With the help of three Falcon 9 boosters, Falcon Heavy is the most capable rocket with its 27 engines. Last Launched: Nov 1, 2022

Starship has yet to launch as a whole but one of it's prototyped halves has been making improvements. Starship is meant to send astronauts to Mars to help with CEO Elon Musk's plans of making humanity multi-planetary.
